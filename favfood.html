It's hard to choose, but here are some of my favorite foods from around the world (in no particular order):

  1. Shawarma with Spicy Garlic Sauce

    Tender marinated meat, flavorful spices, additional veggies and a creamy garlic sauce all wrapped in a warm pita bread. Truly an irresistible Middle Eastern food! (Homemade or not, heavenly taste).

  2. Spring Rolls

    Crispy on the outside, filled with fresh and savory ingredients. A perfect appetizer or light meal. Usually prefer Vietnamese Style.

  3. Butter Chicken with Naan and Chutney

    A classic Indian dish with (preferably mild) creamy tomato-based sauce, tender chicken, and fluffy naan bread. In my opinion, the perfect fullfilling and comfort food.

  4. All Kinds of Tacos and Burritos

    From classic street tacos to loaded burritos, the variety and flavors of Mexican food are endless. Mexicans truly know what they're doing. Nothing beats a street food taco. What explains the attractiveness is the similarities to Middle Eastern food, which hits close to home.

  5. Shakshuka

    Eggs poached in a spicy tomato sauce with peppers and onions. A very hearty and flavorful breakfast or brunch option. One of many traditional dishes of Tunisia (my origin country) that became the standard of breakfasts across the North African/Middle East region and other.

  6. Fried Chicken and Salad

    Crispy, juicy fried chicken paired with a fresh and vibrant salad for a satisfying and balanced meal.

  7. Mediterranean-Style Breakfasts

    Fresh vegetables, olives, cheeses, proteins (usually eggs) and warm pita bread. A healthy, nutritional and delicious way to start the day.

  8. Falafel

    Crispy fried chickpea patties, perfect in a pita with hummus and tahini sauce. A flavorful and satisfying vegetarian option, which I am always excited to have. Also hits home!

  9. Mloukhia

    A unique and flavorful stew made with jute leaves, often served with rice or bread. A true taste of North African cuisine (also country of origin), don't let the unfamiliar look fool you.

  10. Pasta

    From simple spaghetti to creamy dishes, pasta is a versatile yet delicious and easy to make comfort food.

These are just a few of my favorites (that I can recall), but I'm always trying new foods (and yet have to see which will take the top spots)!
